# Project Overview 

QuickBite Express is a Bengaluru-based food-tech startup that faced a major brand crisis in June 2025 due to a viral social media incident involving food safety issues and a week-long delivery outage.

This project analyzes how the crisis affected customer behavior, restaurant performance, delivery operations, and revenue, and proposes a data-driven recovery strategy.

The analysis combines Power BI dashboards, SQL insights, and research-backed secondary analysis to identify the root causes of decline and suggest measurable recovery actions.

# Problem Statement

**In June 2025, QuickBite experienced:**- 

- Massive customer churn and declining daily orders.

- Increased cancellations and poor delivery SLA compliance.

- Negative reviews and a fall in brand trust.

- Restaurant partner drop-offs and revenue loss.

**The management needed insights into:**

- Who was impacted (customers, restaurants, cities).

- What changed (orders, revenue, sentiment).

- Why it happened (root causes).

- How to recover (strategic actions).
